Research Publications CV

I'm a physical oceanographer, working on the interaction between ice and ocean at the University of Gothenburg, Sweden.

I use observations, theory and numerical models to tell stories about the way small interactions can have large impacts on our earth system.

Previously, I was at Woods Hole Oceanographic Institute (WHOI), working on observations of the submesoscale ocean. Prior to WHOI, I did my PhD joint between the British Antarctic Survey and University of Cambridge, Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics.
