About Research Publications CV

I'm a physical oceanographer, and observational mathematician, working on the interaction between ice and ocean at the University of Gothenburg, Sweden.

I use ocean observations, mathematics and numerical models to tell stories about the way small interactions can have large impacts on our earth system.

Previously, I was at Woods Hole Oceanographic Institute (WHOI) for 3 years as a postdoctoral investigator. Prior to WHOI, I completed my PhD joint between the British Antarctic Survey and University of Cambridge.
